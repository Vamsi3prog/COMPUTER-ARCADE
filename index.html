Computer-Arcade

These games can be played only on keyboarded devices.

Read instructions before playing

Pacman

Use side keys to move. On each click you will move a bit. Click on the name to play. Press Ctrl-R to play again.

Use up key to rotate. Use pause button at the top to pause and resume. Click on the name to play. Press Ctrl-R to play again.

Dinorun

Use space key to jump. You should be very carefull and fast. Click on the name to play. Press Ctrl-R to play again.
